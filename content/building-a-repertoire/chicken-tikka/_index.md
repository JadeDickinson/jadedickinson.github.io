Makes two portions, each 386 kcal / 43.9g protein / 23.5g fat.

![Chicken tikka skewers ](chicken-tikka.jpeg)

- 500g skinless boneless chicken thighs
- 1 jalapeno
- 10g garlic
- 30g ginger
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1.5 tsp fine sea salt
- 30ml rice vinegar

1. Peel the garlic, slice or peel the skin off the ginger and roughly chop both, along with the jalapeno.
1. Using a spice grinder or small food processor, whizz all the ingredients up except for the chicken.
1. Fat trim the chicken thighs and chop into roughly 3 by 3 inch squares. Mix in a bowl or ziploc bag with the marinade.
1. Preheat the oven to 180C fan. While it preheats, thread half the chicken onto a metal skewer. Repeat with a second skewer and the rest of the chicken.
1. Cook for 25-30 minutes, rotating each skewer halfway through.

Serve with rice, greek yoghurt and shredded carrots and sliced red onions.
